Bestia

The beasts of the world formed an alliance they called bestia The alliance consists of the beastial races. The Wolven(Wolves), Prideroar(Lions), Avifauna(Birds), and Octan(Octopuses). Bestia is allied with Triticum, and neutral to Odium.

Triticum

The nature dwelling creatures formed an alliance called Triticum The alliance consists of the Elvish, Florus(plant people), Terragon (little dragons), and the Mags(arcane masters). Triticum is allied with Beastia, and neutral to Odium.

/

Odium

The underlings of the world were slumped together as their own. Ever since the great war between the mags and twisear defining how the world will be pieced together. The underlings finally found freedom. This faction consists of the Bakenekos(cat ghosts), Slimelings(slime people), Bugrioche(bug people),and the Twisear(rejected Mags).

Human

When Humans entered this world they wanted nothing to do with the other factions wanted nothing to do with them. After seeing the potential of the world for their own needs the Humans decided that this world was worth too much to share with the other factions of the world. They would rather take conquest against the other factions and try to rule the world they have chosen to take sides with Xatnys.
