Fire

Fire magic consists of the power to create and manipulate fire. A caster of fire can choose to either create and throw flaming projectiles, or envelope their own boides within fire to fight.

Water

Water magic allows a user to bend water to the will of oneself. A water user can choose between brings water out of the seas, or throw themself into the sea for protection.

Earth

Eath magic manifests an affinity to the dirt and rocks around a caster. A caster can either throw bolders at their enemeis, or dig into the earth to find resources and tackle enemeies.

Air

Air magic gives a caster control over the winds. A caster can blow winds at their enemies to blast them away or use the air around them to make oneself lighter.

Electric

Electric magic gives a caster the ability to manifest electrical currents. A caster could charge their own body, or shock their enemies with electricity.

Light

Light magic allows a user to bend light for their own use. The caster can bend the light around then to make them harder to see, or concentrate light into a beam to attack an enemy.

Dark

Dark magic consists of the ability to manipulate the shadow realm. The shadow realm is the parallel dimension where physical shadows live. A caster can either draw power from the shadow realm by finding areas lacking light, or manipulate their own shadow to attack their enemies.

Distortion

Distortion magic distorts the rules of spacetime allowing teleportation, and distorting an enemy's vision. This will grant the power to rip holes in space and warp the caster, or the caster can distort space around an enemy to blind them.

Mind

Mind magic gives power to the mind to a caster of this magic. The power of the mind grants the power to see the future, read the brain, and the power of telekinisis. Mind users can either read the universe or move it.

Necromancy

The power of the undead grants power to reanimate life. Necromancy caster have the power to raise the dead around them, or allow their bodies to become training grounds for their minions to grow strong.

Shapeshifter

A shapeshifter has the ability to manipulate their body structure. A shapeshifter can manually modify their body as they go or absorb the properties of another existing body.

Summoner

A summoner has the ability to summon creatures they met before. A summoner can kill a beast and summon a copy of them to fight later, or they can get the DNA of a creature to transport them later. There is no way to return a creature back to their home using this type of magic.
